Marco Zero

Marco Zero, in Praça Rio Branco, is known as the founding place of the city of Recife and also as the starting point for calculating distances calculated from the city. The place is one of the most important points in the capital of Pernambuco, as it is also a region of strong movement during Carnival. Take advantage of your visit to take a photo with the two landmarks (the old and the new), observe the view of the Sculpture Park and the buildings of the Centro Cultural da Caixa and the Associação Comercial de Pernambuco.
